## Launch Roadmap Summary

### MVP Scope (Target: 12-14 weeks)

**Included:**

- All Must-Have features
- **Pocket Import** (critical timing)
- Limited highlights (10/month free)
- Basic TTS (browser native)
- Dark/light theme

**Not Included:**

- Full-text search (Tier 1)
- Permanent archive (Tier 1)
- Mobile apps (Tier 3)

### Premium at Launch

- Full-text search (Meilisearch)
- Unlimited highlights
- Annotations/notes
- Permanent archive
- Unlimited Pocket import (free tier: 500 articles)

### Fast Follow (Weeks 15-22)

1. API + Webhooks
1. Obsidian integration
1. Keyboard shortcuts
1. Instapaper/Omnivore import
1. AI summaries (BYOK)

### Growth Phase (Weeks 23-34)

1. iOS app (React Native)
1. Android app
1. Advanced filters
1. Smart lists
1. Logseq/Notion integrations
